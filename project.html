Advanced HVAC & Ventilation Commissioning

Complete design, supply, installation, testing, and commissioning of high-efficiency air conditioning and ventilation systems for a large-scale facility.

Project Overview

Climate Crisis LLP is a specialized EPC (Engineering, Procurement, and Construction) contractor dedicated to transforming the HVAC industry. For this project, we took full responsibility for the client's HVAC infrastructure, from the initial heat load calculations and advanced 3D energy modeling to the final commissioning and handover.

Project Challenges

The primary challenge was integrating modern, heavy-duty ventilation systems into an existing structural framework without disrupting ongoing operations. Strict environmental codes required precise energy optimization and perfect airflow balancing across multiple commercial zones.

Our Solution

Our expertise extends beyond basic air quality. Acting as a comprehensive MEP and Interior Solutions partner, we provided end-to-end mechanical, electrical, and plumbing services. We designed a custom chilled-water HVAC system that seamlessly integrated with the interior architecture, ensuring both aesthetic integrity and optimal thermal comfort.

Work Process

  • Initial heat load calculation and energy modeling
  • Design and engineering of custom MEP blueprints
  • Procurement of high-efficiency AC and ventilation units
  • End-to-end mechanical and electrical installation
  • System testing, airflow balancing, and final commissioning

Project Results

The facility now benefits from a state-of-the-art climate control system that significantly reduces energy consumption while maintaining superior indoor air quality. The turnkey handover was completed ahead of schedule.

Safety & Compliance

All engineering, procurement, and construction phases were executed in strict adherence to international ASHRAE standards and local safety regulations, ensuring long-term operational reliability.
